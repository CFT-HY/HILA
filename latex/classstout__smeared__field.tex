\subsubsection*{template$<$typename sun$>$\newline
class stout\+\_\+smeared\+\_\+field$<$ sun $>$}
A stout smeared gauge field built of a standard gauge field. The structure is the same as for the standard and represented gauge fields. \doxylink{classstout__smeared__field_ae76dd114b16b224b30ab736cdc802b38}{refresh()}\+: recalculates the smeared field from the underlying gauge field. \doxylink{classstout__smeared__field_ad230c99c5b59d72fa77d1e339331e319}{add\+\_\+momentum()}\+: transforms a derivative with respect to this field to a derivative with respect to the underlying gauge field and add to the momentum of the gauge field.
